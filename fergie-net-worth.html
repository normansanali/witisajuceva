Fergie | Net Worth & Other Income

Net Worth

The Grammy-winning singer and songwriter Fergie’s net worth as of December 2023 is a staggering $45 million.

Fergie has earned most of her fortune from her successful music career, tons of brand endorsements, lucrative investments, and concert tours.

As mentioned before, Fergie, as part of the hip hop group the Black Eyed Peas, has sold over 80 million.

Likewise, “Big Girls Don’t Cry” became Fergie’s highest-selling and longest-charting single in the US, with 3,833,000 units sold as of August 2012.

Looking at the sales number, it is safe to say that Fergie bagged tons of cash from her solo career as well as being part of the group.

In 2003, the hip-hop group released their third studio album, Elephunk, certified three times platinum by RIAA and seven times platinum by Music Canada.

The album was sold over nine million copies worldwide, amassing a whopping $11.90 million for the group.

Though the money was equally distributed to all the members of the group, it still is a big amount.

Similarly, the fourth studio album by the group Monkey Business sold over nine million copies worldwide and was certified three times platinum by RIAA.

The album sales garnered $17.95 million in total.

In 2006, Fergie’s debut album, The Duchess, was released, which was certified three times platinum by RIAA. This album made $11.9 million for her.

Moreover, Black Eyed Peas album The E.N.D garnered $15.64 million, and the 2020 album The Beginning made $3.82 million for the group.

Other Income

One of the hottest singers in the world, Fergie also earns well from her social media platforms.

She has over 4.6 million followers on her Instagram handle, enabling her to engage in sponsored posts frequently.

Even though the singer is not active in music lately, given her last album released in 2017, she still has her online music streaming platforms continuously generating revenues for her.

Her Spotify platform has over 10 million monthly listeners with over a billion streams, and YouTube has 2.78 million subscribers with nearly two billion views in total.

And we are pretty sure that these two platforms generate a sizable income each month.

According to a popular social media income analysis site, Fergie’s YouTube generates $5k to $80 a month and $60k to $960k each year.

Fergie | Lifestyle & Vacations

Lifestyle

Fergie is pretty rich and lives an affluent lifestyle. Her affluence can be seen in her personality and outfit, no matter how hard she tries to conceal it.

However, it has not always been easy for Fergie as she struggled with crystal methamphetamine addiction which became the reason for her leaving the group in 2001.

During an interview with Time magazine in September 2006, she talked about quitting her crystal meth addiction.

She stated that the meth was the hardest boyfriend she ever had to break up with. Similarly, to overcome the addiction and relax, she used hypnotherapy.

As for her relationships and marriage, Fergie began dating American actor and former fashion model Josh Duhamel in September 2004.

She met Josh when she, along with Black Eyed Peas, filmed an episode of Duhamel’s show Las Vegas.

In a Catholic ceremony, they got engaged in 2007 and married on January 10, 2009. Together they share a son named Axl Jack Duhamel, born in 2013.

However, the love birds separated in 2016. They filed for divorce in June 2019, which was finalized in November of that year.

Workout and Diet

As mentioned before, she looks very hot for her age which can be attributed to the time she puts on her workouts and her diet plans.

Interestingly, Fergie does not have a personal trainer because she does not like someone instructing her what to do.

She is a master of her own and does whatever she wants, whenever she wants. For her exercise, she does squats, walking lunge, step-ups, dancing, and running.

Likewise, she does not include anything processed in her diet and only eats pure and organic food.

Fergie eats small meals every three hours to keep her metabolism up.

For her breakfast, Fergie intake six egg omelets seasoned with hot sauce or salsa, along with two slices of whole-grain toast with two tablespoons of butter.

Fergie |Movies, Endorsements, & Investments

Movies & TV Shows

Aside from contributing to the soundtrack of the films, Fergie has made a handful of appearances in movies and TV shows.

The singer has more than 73 credits as an actress, including her appearances in music videos, TV shows, and movies.

Similarly, she has also voiced animated characters in some blockbuster films, including Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa.

In 1984, she voiced Sally Brown in the TV movie Its Flashbeagle, Charlie Brown.

She reprised her role as Sally in Snoopy’s Getting Married, Charlie Brown in 1985.

Some of her other remarkable TV appearances include Kids Incorporated, California Dreams, Instant Def, The Cleaveland Show, and Fanboy & Chum Chum.

In addition to that, she also made a brief appearance in the American TV sitcom Friends in 1988.

Moreover, Fergie has made appearances in films such as Nine, Planet Terror, Poseidon, and Grindhouse.

Investments

Fergie has invested her money in several lucrative business ventures.

She launched her own handbag in 2007. It was her collaborative effort, Fergie and Kipling.

Similarly, she came up with a line of footwear in 2009 in collaboration with Brown Shoe Company.

The exact figure of her investments has not been revealed, but we can assume that since the team is worth more than $3 billion, even the minority stakeholder is bound to make tens of millions of dollars from the franchise.

Fergie | Career

Fergie, the bombshell, was born in Hacienda Heights, California, on 27 March 1975 to Terri and John, both of whom were school teachers.

She grew up in a Roman Catholic household along with her younger sister Dana.

She went to Mesa Robles Middle School and then attended Glen A. Wilson High School.

Fergie was on a cheerleading squad while in high school. Pretty cool, huh?

Similarly, she was a very good student and made straight A’s, and won spelling bee contests.

Fergie’s parents separated when she was a teenager.
